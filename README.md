# 🌺 Hawaiʻi Action Map

### *Report it. Track it. Fix it.*

**A civic action platform that turns community frustration into real, trackable change — starting on Kauaʻi.**

</div>

---

## 🌊 The Problem

Every island community has *that* Facebook group — on Kauaʻi it's "Rants & Raves." It's where thousands of neighbors go every day to vent about potholes, warn each other about car break-ins, post lost dogs, and share what's happening on the island.

It's the most active place in the community. And it's a **black hole.** A pothole gets 40 angry comments and then vanishes into the feed. All that civic energy evaporates. Nothing actually gets *fixed.*

## 💡 The Idea

**Hawaiʻi Action Map keeps the energy but adds the outcome.** The same daily conversations happen — but the real, actionable issues don't disappear. They get verified by neighbors, backed with photos, organized by AI, and routed to the right county department as trackable cases.

Same island heartbeat. But now it leads somewhere.

---

## ✨ What It Does

Hawaiʻi Action Map is built around **two connected layers**:

### 🗣️ Community — the daily habit
An unfiltered, human, real-time social feed. This is where the island talks — safety alerts, accidents and traffic, lost & found, heads-ups, community notices, and civic issues. Posts feature poster profiles, level badges, reactions, comments, verified tags, trending signals, and a live activity pulse.

### 📍 Pins — the mission & the credibility
The verified, structured, actionable layer. These are real issues the community is acting on:

- **AI-vetted and categorized** — every pin is organized automatically
- **Photo-required** — proof-backed, not just hearsay
- **Routed to the county** — sent to the right department
- **Fundable & volunteer-able** — neighbors can contribute money or show up
- **Status-tracked** — Needs Volunteers → Sent to County → In Progress → Resolved

### 🤖 AI Intake
When you report an issue, AI reads your description, picks the category, sets the urgency, writes clean public and county summaries, detects duplicates, and routes it to the correct department.

### 🏝️ Live Map
A geographic view of what's happening across Kauaʻi, with pins by category.

### 🏆 Profile & Gamification
A membership-card profile with a 10-tier Hawaiian progression system (**Malihini → Aliʻi**), achievements, a journey tracker, contribution stats, and public/private controls.

---

## 🚀 The Star Feature (Coming Soon): The Graduation Pipeline

A rant in **Community** gets confirmed by neighbors and backed with a photo → AI detects and clusters it → it **graduates** into a verified **Pin** → and gets routed to the county as an official case.

**40 scattered complaints become 1 verified, photo-backed, routed case.** That's what turns idle venting into measurable civic wins.

## 🎯 Who It's For

- **Residents** — a free, beautiful app to report issues, stay informed, and organize.
- **Communities** — a healthier, more productive home for the conversations already happening on Facebook.
- **County & local government** — a structured intake pipeline that turns social noise into clean, routable cases (the future paid layer).

## 📦 Getting Started

**Prerequisites:** Node.js 18+ and npm.

```bash
git clone https://github.com/LuanaAguilo/hawaii-action-map.git
cd hawaii-action-map
npm install
npm run dev
```

Then open **http://localhost:3000** in your browser.
